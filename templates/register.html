Registration Guide:

  1. Type the Name of the Person [Ex. Firstname Lastname]
  2. Click the Register Button then wait for the camera view window to Popup while capturing an Image
  3. Everytime you register an Image, It will also update the trained classifier for face recognition
  4. Make sure to do this on a well lighted room or preferably use additional lighting equipments
